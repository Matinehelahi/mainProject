We are Delabs Games

Welcome to the Delabs Playable Layer
Delabs Games is a gaming studio developing and publishing engaging games that aim to lead the mid-core gaming vertical on Telegram and TON. Led by James (Joonmo) Kwon, the former CEO of Nexon and serial entrepreneur with a track record of launching 6+ blockbuster titles, our mission goes beyond creating exceptional games. With our games and our platform, Delabs Games is building a Playable Layer laying the foundation for the next era of gaming where gamers, content creators, and developers thrive together, empowered to explore and shape the future of gaming.
